Prof. Dr. Waleed M. Sh. Alabdraba is a Professor of Environmental Engineering at the College of Engineering, Tikrit University, Iraq. He holds a PhD in Environmental Engineering and has extensive academic and research experience in the field of water and wastewater treatment. His research interests include wastewater treatment processes, environmental protection, activated sludge systems, advanced treatment technologies, and the application of innovative methods for improving water quality.

Prof. Alabdraba has published numerous scientific papers in reputable international journals indexed in Scopus and Web of Science. He actively contributes to postgraduate education through supervising MSc and PhD students, participating in thesis examination committees, and teaching undergraduate and graduate courses in environmental engineering.

His academic activities focus on bridging theoretical knowledge with practical applications, particularly in sustainable water management and environmental pollution control. Through research, teaching, and supervision, he aims to advance environmental engineering practices and support sustainable development initiatives.
